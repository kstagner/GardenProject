About Garden's Galore

Garden's Galore was founded in 1992 by a passionate group of plant-loving people. Today, we are driven to provide to you the most high-quality plants and gardening supplies for even your most abitious gardening plans.

We are proud to be employee-owned. We are gardeners ourselves and have earned our customers' trust by providing high-quality products, expert information, and friendly, personalized service. We understand the products we sell and we use them in our own gardens. We also provide regular training and information services to our customers in order to help make their projects a reality.
